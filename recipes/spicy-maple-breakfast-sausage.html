Spicy Maple Breakfast Sausage

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 2 tablespoons pure maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon dried sage
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • ¼ teaspoon ground marjoram
  • ¼ teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 pinch ground cloves
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps

  1. Combine pork, maple syrup, salt, sage, onion powder, black pepper, red pepper flakes, marjoram, coriander, and cloves in a bowl. Mix well. Form into 10 small patties.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Working in batches, add patties, and fry until no longer pink, and the juices run clear, 3 to 4 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (73 degrees C).
