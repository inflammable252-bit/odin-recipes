Scrambled Egg with Chorizo

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • Â¼ cup Mexican-style chorizo, or more to taste
  • 6 large eggs
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Grease a large nonstick skillet with cooking spray; warm over medium-high heat. Cook and stir chorizo in the hot skillet until browned, about 5 minutes.
  2. Whisk together eggs and salt in a medium bowl until combined; pour over chorizo in the skillet; cook and stir until eggs are set, about 5 minutes.
